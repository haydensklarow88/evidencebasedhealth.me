Creative Brief
Audience Strategy
Build GA4 segments, Meta Custom Audiences, and lookalikes to retarget warm visitors and find new ones.
📈 GA4 Audiences → Google Ads
Build in GA4 and publish to Google Ads for bid observation/remarketing
All Visitors (30 / 60 / 180 days)
Condition: session_start in last 30–180 days. Use as top-level remarketing list in Google Ads.
High-Intent Learners
Viewed GLP-1/peptide/TRT pages AND fired engaged_session or scroll_depth. Best for Google Search bid boost (+30%).
Leads
Fired lead event. Publish to Google Ads as a conversion audience and seed for Performance Max.
Open GA4 Audiences ↗ Google Ads Audiences ↗
In GA4 → Admin → Audiences, create each segment above. Then link your GA4 property to Google Ads (Admin → Google Ads Links). Audiences publish within 24–48 hours.
🎯 Meta Custom Audiences
Website visitors segmented by topic for retargeting + lookalikes
All Website Visitors
All PageView events — 30, 60, and 180-day windows. Broad retargeting base.
GLP-1 / Peptide / TRT Interest
ViewContent with content_category IN (GLP1, PEPTIDE, TRT). URL-based or Pixel custom event match.
Engaged Visitors
Fired EngagedVisitor or DeepScroll custom events (45s+ or 75%+ scroll). Warmest retargeting pool.
Leads (Subscribers)
Fired Lead event. Seed for 1–2% Lookalike Audiences.
Meta Audiences Manager ↗
"Create All" will create these as Website Custom Audiences via the Meta Marketing API for the selected client's ad account. Lookalike creation is manual in Ads Manager after audiences accumulate ≥100 people.
🔁 Lookalike Strategy
Meta 1–2% lookalikes from warm audiences
Leads Lookalike (1%)
Seed: Lead event audience. Most valuable — finds people similar to your actual subscribers.
Engaged Topic Lookalike (1–2%)
Seed: GLP-1/Peptide/TRT audience. Finds people likely to engage with medical education content.
Email List Lookalike
Upload hashed subscriber list to Meta (via Custom Audience → Customer List) then create lookalike. Requires GDPR opt-in or US CAN-SPAM consent.
Create Lookalike in Meta ↗
Lookalikes need ≥ 100 source audience members. Start at 1%, then expand to 2–3% once you have conversion data.
📋 Recommended Campaign Structure
Per client — education-first, two-funnel model
Meta — Cold/Top-of-Funnel
Objective: Traffic or Engagement
Targeting: Lookalikes (Leads 1%) + Advantage+ broad
Creative: Education-only → drives to EBH article or partner education page

Meta — Warm Retargeting
Objective: Leads
Targeting: GLP-1/Peptide/TRT visitors + Engaged Visitors
Creative: "Take our 2-min eligibility quiz with [Partner]"

Google — Search
Targeting: Symptom/lifestyle keywords (not drug terms)
Audiences: High-Intent Learners + Leads as Observation +30%
Assets: Educational headlines

Google — Performance Max
Seed: Leads audience + email subscriber customer list
Assets: Education-first visuals + headlines

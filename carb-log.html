Tips for best results
  • Be specific about brands & portions � e.g. "1 cup Chobani plain greek yogurt" works better than "yogurt"
  • List one food at a time for combo meals � e.g. log the biscuit and gravy separately
  • Include cooking method when it matters � "white rice cooked" vs "white rice dry"
  • For restaurant food, name the restaurant � e.g. "Chipotle chicken bowl with rice and black beans"
This tool is experimental � carb estimates may be off, especially for homemade dishes or unfamiliar brands. Always double-check values before any medical decision. We're actively working to improve accuracy.
